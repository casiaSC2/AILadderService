What is StarCraft2 AI Ladder?

StarCraft2 AI Ladder is a StarCraft2 bot Ladder platform.

How can I use it?

  1. Write your building order in the json file.
  2. Sign up in the "Submit Bot" page and submit your json file.
  3. Your bot will play with other bots automatically.
  4. Just wait and you will see the result in the "Ladder" page.
